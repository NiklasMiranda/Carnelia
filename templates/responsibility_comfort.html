Tailored to your comfort

Striving towards comfort for everyone

As a modern lingerie brand, we aim to adhere to the highest standards and we take on the responsibility of making lingerie for everyone. Our purpose is to fight unrealistic beauty standards, unhealthy body images and standardized ideas of what defines femininity. At Carnelia we have designed new technologies to ensure the support needed for the different sizes we offer. The three size ranges come with different backs.

Classic

The classic back is for the lowest size range. It’s well-known and well-loved and is a design that works great for bodies that don’t require maximum support. It has adjustable straps to ensure a perfect fit and extra lines of hooks to adjust the circumference.

Signature

This version comes with the signature size range and offers extra support to those with a medium sized chest and moderate need of support. The back straps work as a rope and pulley, providing four contact points instead of two thus ensuring an even weight distribution.

This eases the strain on the shoulder straps and allows a more comfortable wear. The straps are connected via the ring at the shoulder blade area. This ring connects the shoulder strap and the two straps that connect to the back of the underbust band.

Supreme

This version comes with the supreme size range. This option is for those with a large chest in need of maximum support. The straps are much wider than the classic and signature, allowing the weight from the front to be more comfortably dispersed on the shoulders.

The underbust band is thicker, ensuring even pressure dispersal on the ribcage, allowing the bra to feel more comfortable for someone with a need for utmost support.
