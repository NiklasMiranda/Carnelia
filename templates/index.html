Eco-consciousness in lingerie

Carnelia represents a new way of crafting lingerie, offering a more inclusive and comfortable design. The core of lingerie is making people feel beautiful, confident, and at ease in their body. This goes beyond their size, gender, or how they define their femininity.

Our first collection, The Four Seasons consists of 4 sets: Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter. The idea behind the collection came from the similarities between the changes of the body and the changes of the seasons.
